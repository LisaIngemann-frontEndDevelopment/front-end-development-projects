NASA TV

Watch NASA TV that was originally created for NASA managers and engineers to be provided with real-time video of missions. They have operated a television service since the start of space programs to archive the data and provide media outlets with video footage.

NASA TV airs many educational programs, and provides live coverage of many manned missions including the International Space Station, robotic missions, and domestic and international launches.
