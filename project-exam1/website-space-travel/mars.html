Images Taken By Mars Rovers

Look at images taken by the different Mars rovers: Curiosity, Opportunity and Spirit.

Curiosity was launched near the end of November 2011 and landed on Mars at the start of August 2012. Its goal is to determine if Mars was ever able to support microbial life. Opportunity was launched in the start of July 2003 and landed near the end of January 2004. It stayed operational longer than expected and stopped working in June 2018. Spirit was launched around the same time as Opportunity and landed on the other side of Mars. This rover got stuck and lost communications near the end of March 2010.
